HELLO

Sit back and relax now. Don’t be in a hurry. Don’t close the browser too quickly, because what you are about to experience will blow your mind. You will walk through a terrible design, horrible layout, structure, and architecture of this website. The colors will be terribly imbalanced and confusing. The only honest and true thing on this website is the content—we got that right. Everything else is unfortunate. Yet, it is done with great style, passion, and dedication. Despite the fact that the design is really, really bad, it is unique—like you and me. We believe we have controlled the chaos. We made it harmonious and ordered amidst all this mess. We are helping your eye flow through the text, from piece to piece, from letter to letter. And we did that intentionally. You might hate this design, but you can’t stay neutral. It is impossible to create a design that everyone will love, but it is possible to create one that everyone will remember.

Remember this website because it will leave its footprint in the history of the internet. How foolish, but at the same time brave, was the creator of it. He made every possible mistake from design theory, but he made them with a bang.

Have fun, and welcome to Joker.
